Getting Help#

When you see a videocam, click on it to watch the video

When you see a photo, click on it to view the screenshot

If you need Help, or can't find what you are looking for:

Clicking headset opens an online meeting with video for a live walkthrough.

Clicking call starts a phone call to a Business Software Design Tech Support Team Member.

Clicking mail emails the Business Software Design tech team.

Clicking forum opens a live chat with a Business Software Design Tech Support Team Member.

Hours of operation#

What time is Tech Support available?#

Business Software Design commit to providing Live Technical Support between the hours of

06:00-17:00 Monday to Friday as a minimum.

Business Software Design nearly always have tech staff working throughout the night and even weekends.

On the rare occasions the tech team have all signed off overnight, the live help buttons will disappear.

You will have access to mail and a new icon videocam , opens our screen recorder plugin which

  1. records what you were trying to do and allows you to explain in plain 'non-geek speak'.
  2. creates a support ticket, and
  3. emails tech support staff with the ticket, the issue and the recording,
  4. Tech Support will reply ASAP.
